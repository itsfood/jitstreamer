A JIT enabler that works anywhere you have WiFi

Getting Started

Step One

Download ZeroTier One from the App Store. Add a new connection and type 017c0c264be7ec5b. Change DNS to Network DNS and click Add Network. Keep everything else default.

Getting Started

Step Two

Download the JIT shortcut here , and run it while connected to the ZeroTier VLAN. Run it to get a code.

Getting Started

Step Three

Download PAIR for your computer from this website . Plug in your device and follow the instructions to generate your pairing file. Enter the code from the shortcut when prompted.

Note: You only have to do this once!

Getting Started

Step Four

Choose the app you want to enable JIT for from the menu that pops up.

Note: You must be on the VLAN over WIFI to use the shortcut!

Getting Started

Profit!

Now you can access your favorite JIT-enabled apps on the go, wherever you have WIFI.
