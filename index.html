Quick Setup

Step One

Open ZeroTier One and add a new connection. Enter "017c0c264be7ec5b" in the Network ID field. Change the DNS to "Network DNS" and tap Add Network. Keep everything else default.

Quick Setup

Step Two

Generate a pairing code. Remain on the ZeroTier VLAN while you generate your code. Copy or make note of it, you will need this in the next step.

Will JitStreamer ever be updated to work over Cellular?

Unfortunately, no. Apple has blocked JIT from being enabled over cellular networks. This is built-in limitation of iOS.
