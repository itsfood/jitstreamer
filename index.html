Getting Started

What You'll Need

  • ZeroTier One from the App Store.
  • The JIT Streamer Shortcut for the Shortcuts app.
  • JIT Streamer Pair app for a computer. You will only need this once.

Getting Started

Step One

Open ZeroTier One, and add a new connection. Enter "017c0c264be7ec5b" in the Network ID field. Change the DNS to "Network DNS" and tap Add Network. Keep everything else default.

Getting Started

Step Two

Run the JIT Streamer Shortcut while connected to the ZeroTier VLAN to get a pairing code. You will need this in the next step.

Getting Started

Step Three

On your computer, open JIT Streamer Pair. Plug in your device and follow the instructions to generate your pairing file. Enter the code from the shortcut when prompted.

Note: You only have to do this once!

Getting Started

Step Four

Back on your phone, choose the app you want to enable JIT for from the popup menu.

Note: You must be on the VLAN over WIFI to use the shortcut!

Getting Started

Profit!

Now you can access your favorite JIT-enabled apps on the go, wherever you have WIFI.
